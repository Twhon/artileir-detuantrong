The Maker

A dream that refused
to stay quiet

Maison Geminé was born from a single conviction: that extraordinary watchmaking shouldn't require an extraordinary budget. Each piece begins as a sketch, evolves through weeks of refinement, and ends as something meant to last generations.

Based in an independent studio, every watch is assembled, decorated, and finished by hand. No shortcuts. No compromise. Just an obsessive attention to the details that make the difference between a watch that tells time and one that tells a story.
